About Our Company

We understand that technology is evolving at a fast pace and keeping up-to-date can be a daunting task. Our goal is to help you take a step back and evaluate what can be a solid technology versus what could be a fad. Our success is the direct reflection of your success and our work together.

Our expertise lie in product development covering software and hardware aspects of each project. We also help resources achieve a level of mastery of product knowledge that will further streamline development. This mastery can be achieved through our training services.

Our Process

Discovery

At Iotopia Solutions, the discovery process is the first step to a sucessful project. The scope and depth of research and inquiry will differ from project to project, but the results are the same: valuable data. The more information that is gathered, interpreted, and understood, the more prepared we will be to complete the project on budget and on target.

Design

Drawing from the information gathered from the discovery phase, it’s time to determine the look and feel of the application or site. Along with wireframing and prototyping, the overall visual style will most likely be determined by the visual brand of the organization. The goal will be to connect the Web with all other forms of the organization’s communications.

Development

At Iotopia Solutions Inc. we build websites and apps that perform. We believe the best technology should be seamless to design. We apply and innovate upon open source technologies to deliver solutions that perform for your company and your users. With a full team of developers working on cutting-edge solutions, we customize interactive development for all of the project's needs.

Deployment

Sometimes a bad production deployment can ruin all the effort invested in a development process. Having a solid deployment workflow can become one of the greatest advantages to the team. What seems like a simple thing can actually be quite complex. At Iotopia Solutions Inc., we will safely deploy your application through Development, Staging and Production enviroments.

What we do

Technology Evaluation

To ensure that we provide the necessary technology and that we don't overstay our welcome (completing the project in a timely manner), we provide a service in which we take a discovery period to evaluate the current technology stack, the shortcomings, and the future needs. The end result is a report that will outline the concerns and provide a detailed roadmap for the future.

Product Design & Development

Navigating the ever changing technology landscape can be daunting. Our goal is to wade through the fad and the latest shiny toy and ensure that our partners select the proper toolset that will fit their needs for the immediate and near future. Building modularly designed components (Atomic Design) is where we start and continue this principle through the end of project. Set up an appointment to see how we can help you.

Startup support

To visualize one's idea on a digital medium can be challenging. We enjoy helping startups visualize their dreams. We also assist in selecting the proper technology that will be helpful in the entire journey, a lasting framework that will go beyond the prototype phase.
