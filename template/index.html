About Our Company

We understand that technology is evolving in a fast pace and keeping up can be daunting task. Our goal is to help you take a step back, take a breath, evaluate what is solid technology versus what could possibly be a fad. Our success is the direct reflection of your success with our partnership.

Our Process

Discovery

At Iotopia Solutions The discovery process is the first step to a sucessful project. The Scope and depth of research and inquiry will differ from project to project, but the results are the same: valuable data. The more information you gather, interpret, and comprehend, the more prepared you will be to execute a site on budget and on target.

Design

Drawing from the information gathered from the discovery phase, it’s time to determine the look and feel of your application or site. Along with wireframing and prototyping, the overall visual style will most likely be determined by the visual brand of the organization; the goal being to connect the Web with all other forms of the organization’s communications.

Development

At Iotopia Solutions Inc. we build websites and apps that perform. We believe the best technology is seamless to design. We apply and innovate upon enterprise or open source technologies to deliver solutions that perform for your company and your users. With a full team of developers working on cutting-edge solutions, we customize interactive development for all you needs.

Deployment

Sometimes a bad production deployment can ruin all the effort you invested in a development process. Having a solid deployment workflow can become one of the greatest advantages of your team. What seems like a simple thing can actually be quite complex. At Iotopia Solutions Inc., we will safely deploy your application though Development, Staging and Production enviroments.
